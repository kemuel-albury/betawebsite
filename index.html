We are the FreedomBytes and we specialize in making the world a better place. Climate change defines a change in average conditions such as temperature and rainfall. Global climate change refers to the long-term average shifts around the entire world. Greenhouse gases exist naturally and by preventing some of the sun's warmth from reflecting into space and rendering Earth livable, are crucial to the survival of humans and millions of other living beings.
But after more than a century and a half of industrialization, deforestation, and large-scale agriculture, atmospheric greenhouse gas concentrations have increased to record levels that have not been seen in three million years. In the last 650,000 years there have been seven cycles of glacial advance and retreat, with the abrupt end of the last ice age about 11,700 years ago marking the beginning of the modern climate era and of human civilization.

Services We Provide

Carbon Footprint Calculator

When we do things such as disposing waste, driving a car or even using electricity these all create something called greenhouse gas emissions.
When these emissions are added up, it results in a household's carbon footprint.
This calculator will give you the total of your footprint, starting from your home energy to your waste and transportation.

Hurricane Tracker

The Hurricane Tracker will allow persons to follow hurricanes or tropical storms that are within there location and will include updates on its conditions.

Data Chart

The Data Chart will store the total amount of pollution, carbon emissions, hurricane intensity and associated amount of damage done.

Shelter Locator

It will let persons notify and even assist persons in finding the nearby hurricane shelters.

Wet Bulb Thermometer

The Wet bulb thermometer will automatically show the weathers condition. That will include the wind speed, wind pressure, temperature and more.

Sea-Level Gauge

With the Sea-Level Gauge, it will monitor the seas average level yearly to get an idea about the progression of climate change. This will be mainly for Deep Waters.
